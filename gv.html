Green Vision is the concept of environmental social network where you can share natural environmental disasters. This application was created by me and my team in just 36 hours on the Hackathon.

Client

Hackathon

Location

Whitby, Ontario

Contribution

Main contributed in UI, logo design, and custom components. I also participated in ideation, sketching and wireframing.

Setting

Goal

During our brainstorming, we decided to design and develop an app that raises social awareness of environmental issues like pollution and natural disasters. Although environmental issues have a lot of public attention, some important events are often overshadowed by another because of the lack of social input.

Defining

Solution

Before designing the application, it is essential to establish primary features that will keep users engaged.

01. App Category

To get users attention on an environmental subject we decided to make a social network app so people can have direct involvement. Many enthusiasts would like to have an application where they can share the information they discovered.

02. User Engagement

Having people do regular text posts is boring and repetitive. I researched current trends and discovered that currently, the most favorite way to share information is visual. Instagram lets users share information in the form of pictures and videos which adds personality and visual exposition to every post. It suits the idea since events like natural cataclysms and human pollutuion are very visual(sometimes spectacular).

03. Information Discovery

Environmental events can happen in a different part of the globe. We decided to make not only feed but also an interactive map so users can find events that occur in the near area or in another part of the world. What if there is a volcano eruption in two street from your house? And you loved that coffeeshop.

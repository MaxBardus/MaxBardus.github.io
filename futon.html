Futon

Android App

The Futon is a simple mobile messaging application that you have conversations with your friends. The app was made in the scope of the academic project for Android Application Development course.

With Novo, you can track your fast with precision. The app will record if you decided to interrupt your fast, skipped it and your fast start and end time with exact date. All features are wrapped in modern, intuitive design powered by Google's latest Material Design 2.0.

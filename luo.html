Luo

IOS Application Concept

Role
UI/UX Designer
Design Tools
InVision Studio, InVision
Client
BrainStation project
Location
Toronto, ON

Luo is designed to help people find parking that is free (or with some conditions applied) and close to the location they are going to spend time in. Luo takes its architecture inspiration from social networks apps. Thus, all information in the app posted and shared by users themselves.

Green Vision

Android App

Green Vision is the concept of environmental social network where you can share natural environmental disasters. This application was created by me and my team in just 36 hours on the Hackathon

The App lets you discover latest environment-related events through horizontal feed or interactive map. You can open up events that have been posted to view detail information and upvote it so it will receive more attention from the app users

You can share the environmental events you discovered. The app interface lets you quickly make a post or choose it from your gallery. So if you see a volcano eruption in Iceland, take a phone and make the shot. Just don't get to close.

Novo

Android/IOS Application

Role
UI/UX Designer
Design Tools
Sketch, InVision, Material Theming
Development
React Native
Team
Pavle Boraniev - Mobile Developer

Novo is an intermittent fasting app that lets you track your fast with no distractions on extra useless features. The app enables you to monitor your fasting time, so you don't need to worry if you missed prescheduled fast or overdid/underdid it.

Client

Personal/Collaborative

Location

Toronto, Ontario

Contribution

Designed Android version of the app with second gen of Material Design and custom components.

analyzing

Problem

Currently, there is no prominent intermittent fasting tracker available in Google Play and App Store. Moreover, apps that are available often require a monthly subscription to enable tracking feature or contain many ads which disturb user experience.

Conducting

Research

In order to design experience that mathes target audience, I analyzed the market and its users.

01. Competitive Analysis

During research of the existing competition, I discovered that there is currently no fitness application designed specifically for tracking fasts in Google Play. Based on this information it was decided to develop an Android version first.

02. For Who

Our primary users will be people who practice fitness lifestyle consistently and have an interest in tech believing that will help them achieve their goals. Generally, they have a good understanding of how to use different devices and apps which opens the door to design an app in a more creative fashion.

03. Design Style

I designed the app merging custom style to provide a unique look and feel to the interface with second gen of Google's Material Design to ensure familiarity and consistency of user experience.

Usability

Intuitive Testing

To test if the app has a clear and understandable interface I decided to do the "Intuitive Testing." The basic idea of this type of testing is to analyze if the user can understand what each event trigger in the app will do before the actual interaction. I disabled buttons that has specific functionality and questioned several users about what they expect to see after clicking them.

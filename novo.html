Novo

Android & IOS Apps

Novo is an intermittent fasting app that lets you track your fast with no distractions on extra useless features. The app enables you to monitor your fasting periods, so you don't need to worry if you missed prescheduled fast or overdid/underdid it.

With Novo, you can track your fast with precision. The app will record if you decided to interrupt your fast, skipped it and your fast start and end time with exact date. All features are wrapped in modern, intuitive design powered by Google's latest Material Design 2.0.

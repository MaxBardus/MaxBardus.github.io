La Liairson

UI Concept

La Liairson is a premium boutique app concept based on the second generation of Material Design. While Material was always playful design guideline, this concept shows that it can be used to create an expansive look for software.

Design combines the simplicity of white color with passion vibe of Amber. There is also a nice contrast between rounded corners of the logo and sharp conners of the buttons
